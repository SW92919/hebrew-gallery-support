Privacy Policy for Hebrew Gallery

Last updated: 18 Dec 2025

Introduction

Hebrew Gallery ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Photo Library Access

Hebrew Gallery requests access to your photo library to:

  • Display your photos organized by Hebrew calendar dates
  • Convert photo creation dates to Hebrew dates
  • Organize photos by Jewish holidays

Important: We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos to any external servers. All processing happens locally on your device.

Location Data (Optional)

If you choose to enable location services, we use your location to:

  • Calculate accurate sunset times for "Erev" date adjustments
  • Improve Hebrew date conversion accuracy

Location data is processed locally and never transmitted to external servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Organize your photos by Hebrew calendar dates
  • Identify photos taken on Jewish holidays
  • Provide accurate date conversions
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

All data processing occurs locally on your device. We do not:

  • Store your photos on external servers
  • Transmit your photos over the internet
  • Share your personal data with third parties
  • Collect personal information beyond what's necessary for app functionality

Third-Party Services

Hebrew Gallery does not integrate with third-party services that collect personal data. We use only Apple's built-in frameworks for photo library access and date calculations.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke photo library access at any time through iOS Settings
  • Disable location services if you prefer
  • Delete the app and all associated data

Children's Privacy

Hebrew Gallery does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
