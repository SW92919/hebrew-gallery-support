Frequently Asked Questions

How do I grant photo access?

Go to Settings > Hebrew Gallery > Photos and select "All Photos"

How do I process my photos?

Open the app and tap "Process Photos" on the home screen.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings tab > Language and select your preferred language.
