Frequently Asked Questions

How do I grant photo library access?

When you first open the app, you'll be prompted to grant photo access. You can also go to iOS Settings > Hebrew Gallery > Photos to change permissions.

How do I process my photos?

Open the app and tap "Process Photos" on the home screen. The app will analyze your photos and organize them by Hebrew dates.

How do I change the language?

Go to the Settings tab in the app and select your preferred language (English or Hebrew).

What is Erev (evening) mode?

Erev mode includes photos taken after sunset on the evening before a Hebrew date. Enable this in Settings if you want more accurate Hebrew date matching.

How do I search for photos by holiday?

Go to the Holidays tab to browse all Jewish holidays, or use the Search tab to find photos from specific holidays.

Privacy

Hebrew Gallery processes all photos on your device. No photos are uploaded or shared. Your privacy is our priority.
